Brief description of participant with no personal identifiers:
A 31-year-old pregnant participant exposed to anti-epileptic medication ZNV-6 during the first trimester delivered a neonate with a structural cardiac anomaly identified shortly after birth.

Brief description of the nature of the serious adverse event:
The pregnancy had been monitored due to maternal seizure disorder. Fetal anomaly scan had shown possible cardiac abnormality, later confirmed after delivery. The newborn was diagnosed with congenital heart defect requiring specialist neonatal cardiology evaluation. Maternal condition remained stable throughout pregnancy.
